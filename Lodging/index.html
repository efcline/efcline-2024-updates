Hampton Inn address is: 244 North Avenue NW, Atlanta, GA 30313 If you are flying in to Atlanta Hartfield-Jackson Airport, the Hampton Inn is easily accessible by taking the MARTA train from the airport.

  1. Take the MARTA train to the Midtown station (there will be two lines of MARTA at the airpot, both go to Midtown).
  2. After getting off the train, take a Tech Trolley (free Georgia Tech campus bus) to the Transit Hub station (the last stop of Trolley).
  3. After getting off the Trolley, walk along the Tech Pkwy to North Ave. street (only a couple minutes).
