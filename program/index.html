1:30 - 2:45pm

 

Data Curation Network

Jennifer Darragh (Duke)

Joel Herndon (Duke)

  • Why Data Curation?
  • Launched in May of 2016, the Data Curation Network is an ambitious, multi-organizational attempt to harness the data curation expertise of a network of data interests representing libraries, repositories, and data organizations. This presentation explores the rationale for the DCN, its current work, and future goals while sharing some strategies that the DCN employs to curate data in the network.
  • The presentation will conclude with a data curation exercise.

Closing session

Dr. Subhrajit Guhathakurta (Georgia Tech)

  • Attendees will have the opportunity to discuss with Dr. Guhathakurta about how library data services can provide:
